1.Haunted House Night: Transform your restaurant into a haunted house for a night.

2.Murder Mystery Dinner: Host a murder mystery dinner where guests can participate in solving a spooky crime while enjoying their meal. Provide clues throughout the evening, and reveal the solution at the end.

3.Horror Movie Marathon: Organize a horror movie marathon night where guests can watch classic horror films while indulging in themed snacks and drinks. Consider having special screenings of cult classics or hosting themed trivia during intermissions.

4.Halloween Costume Party: Throw an epic Halloween costume party with a costume contest, themed decorations, and spooky music. Offer prizes for the best costumes and encourage guests to come dressed as their favorite horror movie characters.

5.Ghoulish Gala: Host a fancy dinner event with a ghoulish twist. Serve gourmet dishes with creepy names like "Eyeball Pasta" or "Witch's Brew Soup," and decorate the restaurant with elegant yet eerie decor.
